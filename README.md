# Career Assistant Platform

An all-in-one platform to help you craft powerful cold emails and prepare effectively for interviews.

---

## Cold Email Generator

Generate personalized cold emails tailored to your goals and communication style.

```
 Cold Email Generator
  ├── 4 email types: Application, Networking, Referral, Follow-up
  ├── 4 tones: Professional, Friendly, Confident, Enthusiastic
  ├── Alternative subject lines (click to swap)
  ├── Copy to clipboard
  ├── Personalization tips
  └── Regenerate option
```

### ✨ Features

* Choose from multiple email types depending on your purpose.
* Adjust tone to match your personality or company culture.
* Instantly swap subject lines for better open rates.
* Copy generated emails with one click.
* Get actionable personalization tips.
* Regenerate improved variations anytime.

---

## Interview Preparation

Comprehensive interview preparation across aptitude, HR, technical, and behavioral rounds.

```
🎯 Interview Preparation
  ├── 🧮 Aptitude Test
  │   ├── MCQ format with 4 options
  │   ├── Auto-grading on submit
  │   ├── Score + percentage + grade
  │   ├── Correct/wrong visual feedback
  │   └── Detailed explanations
  │
  ├── 👥 HR Interview
  │   ├── Common HR questions
  │   ├── Personalized suggested answers
  │   ├── "What they look for" insights
  │   └── Common mistakes to avoid
  │
  ├── 💻 Technical Interview
  │   ├── Role-specific technical questions
  │   ├── Model answers
  │   ├── Key points to mention
  │   └── Follow-up questions
  │
  ├── 🎯 Behavioral (STAR)
  │   ├── STAR-method structured answers
  │   ├── Situation → Task → Action → Result
  │   ├── Personalized using user's experience
  │   └── Question variations
  │
  └── 🎤 Mock Interview
      ├── Full interview flow simulation
      ├── Mixed HR + Technical + Behavioral
      ├── Time suggestions per question
      ├── Scoring criteria
      └── Overall interview tips
```

### 💡 What You Get

* Practice tests with instant scoring and feedback.
* Structured answers using proven frameworks like STAR.
* Role-specific technical preparation.
* Realistic mock interviews with performance insights.
* Clear guidance on what interviewers actually evaluate.
